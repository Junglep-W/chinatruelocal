First Time in China?

Start with these 3 essentials before you fly.

1. Payment Apps are Mandatory ‚Üí

Visa/Mastercard works rarely directly. You MUST link them to Alipay or WeChat Pay before arrival.

2. You need a VPN or Roaming ‚Üí

Google Maps, Gmail, and Instagram are blocked. We recommend buying an eSIM (like Holafly) or a reliable VPN.

3. Don't trust Apple Maps entirely ‚Üí

For navigation, Apple Maps is okay, but Amap (Gaode) is better if you can read a little Chinese. We explain how to use it in our guides.
